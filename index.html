Short Biography

Dr. Jianke Zhu is currently an associate professor in School of Computer Science at Zhejiang University. He received his Ph.D degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Chinese University of Hong Kong. He was a postdoc in BIWI computer vision lab of ETH Zurich. .

Research interest

My research focuses on computer vision and multimedia information retrieval. I have worked on a variety of novel techniques and their applications to nonrigid shape recovery, image alignment, 3D surface reconstruction, multimedia search and annotation, augmented reality and digit entertainment. I am especially interested in developing new image alignment techniques, which are fundamental to many vision and multimedia applications.
