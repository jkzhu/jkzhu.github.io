Terms of Releasing Datasets and Implementation

We are happy to release our datasets and source codes so that peer researchers can reconstruct the experimental results in our papers. Furthermore, we would be very delighted if our testbed and implementation would be beneficial to the community for developing new techniques and algorithms.

The author is not responsible for implications from the use of them. If you use either the datasets or the code in your scientific work, please cite our related papers.

Bug Reports

About Asking Questions

Please be reminded that all the datasets/source codes are released as the way they are. We would be delighted if you could use the datasets in your research and understand the implementation, and adapt it for your own purposes. If you cannot understand it, please kindly write your own codes. Our codes should be regarded as "freeware", for which the authors do not hold any responsibility. Emails soliciting explanation may be ignored.

Finally, we would like to thank researchers in the community for their understanding, and sincerely hope our work would be beneficial for others.
